# ig_single / data-callout archetype

Composition emphasizes a single number or short claim; minimalist
background; brand palette restrained.

## Prompt template

A minimalist composition emphasizing a single bold data point or
short claim. Background: solid or subtly-textured surface in
{brand_palette_hex} (use HEX codes). The focal area is empty
whitespace where the number/claim will be Pillow-composited after.
{subject} appears as a small supporting visual element in a lower
third — NOT the focal point; the number is. Square aspect ratio 1:1,
1080×1080. Style: editorial, restrained, B2B-friendly.

## Slots

- `{subject}`           — small supporting visual (icon, abstract anchor)
- `{brand_palette_hex}` — comma-separated HEX codes

## When to use

- "47% of marketers" callout-style posts
- Single-number-driven announcements
- Editorial data callouts
