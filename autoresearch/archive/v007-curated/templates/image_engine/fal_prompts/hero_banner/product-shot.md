# hero_banner / product-shot archetype

Single-glance comprehension. Hero shot of product or named subject;
F-pattern composition; headline + CTA Pillow-composited after.

## Prompt template

A wide hero banner photograph of {subject} in {lighting}, optimized
for single-glance comprehension. Composition: F-pattern — primary
subject in upper-left or center-left; clear focal hierarchy;
abundant whitespace on the right third for Pillow-composited
headline + subhead + CTA button. Brand palette dominant:
{brand_palette_hex} (HEX codes, accents only — no rendered text in
the image). Aspect ratio 16:9, exactly 1600×900. NO logos, NO
text overlays — those Pillow-composited after. Style: editorial,
spacious, brand-anchored.

## Slots

- `{subject}`           — focal subject (product, person, named scene)
- `{lighting}`          — e.g., "soft window", "studio strobe", "natural"
- `{brand_palette_hex}` — accent colors

## When to use

- Product launch hero banners
- gofreddy.ai landing-page heroes
- Vertical-specific hero shots (Klinika treatment room, DWF office)
