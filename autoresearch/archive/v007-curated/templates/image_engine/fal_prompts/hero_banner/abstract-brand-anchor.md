# hero_banner / abstract-brand-anchor archetype

For brand-anchor heroes where no specific product/scene anchors the
shot. Geometric or atmospheric composition keyed to brand palette;
text composited prominently.

## Prompt template

A wide editorial brand-anchor banner. Composition: an abstract
visual anchor — geometric or atmospheric — keyed to
{brand_palette_hex}. Avoid generic AI tells: NO floating 3D blobs,
NO three-icon trios, NO lime+purple gradients. Use the brand's
specific palette via HEX codes; treat the visual as a backdrop for
heavy Pillow-composited typography. Left two-thirds of the frame:
empty whitespace for headline + subhead + CTA. Right third:
{subject} as a textural anchor (abstract pattern, brand-aligned
icon family, or atmospheric image). Aspect ratio 16:9, exactly
1600×900. NO rendered text.

## Slots

- `{subject}`           — abstract anchor concept (≤8 words)
- `{brand_palette_hex}` — brand HEX codes

## When to use

- Brand-positioning landing hero
- Service-page hero where no product photograph exists
- B2B SaaS abstract heroes (within brand discipline)
